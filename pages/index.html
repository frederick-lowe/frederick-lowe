Who I Am

I am Frederick Lowe: Rad Dad, Full Time Nomad, and Senior Systems Architect. If you're curious what these pillars of my personal mythology mean, read on.

Rad Dad

Some dads like to work. Some dads like to watch TV. Some dads like to drink beer. I like all of those things (in balance) but what really lights me up is hiking, biking, and adventuring with my family (and leaving no trace in the places we visit).

Full Time Nomad

There's no way to do more adventuring with your family than to go camping with them. So three years ago, we shed our flaming worldly possessions and bought a 5th-wheel to see the world. As we roll into our fourth year there's no end in sight.
